# Ernst & Young / EY (ernst-and-young)

Ernst & Young (EY) is one of the Big Four professional services organizations, operating as a global network of legally separate and independent member firms branded under "EY" and coordinated by Ernst & Young Global Limited (EYG). EY reports roughly 400,000 people spanning approximately 150 countries, anchored by the stated purpose "Building a better working world." EY delivers across Assurance, Consulting, Strategy & Transactions (EY-Parthenon), Tax, Law, Technology, Managed Services, and People & Workforce Services, plus the cross-cutting EY Private and EY Sustainability practices. EY does not publish a public developer API or corporate developer portal; the surface profiled here covers the institutional, research-publishing, and open-source blockchain footprints.

## APIs

No public developer APIs are published at the corporate level. APIs that EY builds inside engagements — embedded in EY Nexus deployments, surfaced through EY.ai, or shipped as part of an EY Managed Services arrangement — live with each client and are not exposed on an EY-branded developer surface. The only first-party EY GitHub organization that is publicly discoverable is **EYBlockchain** (`github.com/EYBlockchain`, ~28 public repositories, ~160 followers, website `blockchain.ey.com`), which hosts EY's open-source zero-knowledge cryptography and privacy-preserving blockchain work.

## EY Insights (Research Surface)

EY Insights is the firm's research-publishing program, organized around topic areas — **AI, Finance / CFO Agenda, Geostrategy, People and Workforce, Risk, Sustainability, Technology, EY Transformation** — and shipped through articles, recurring report series, EY Podcasts, and EY Webcasts. The Insights surface positions itself as "the insights you need to make better decisions today, to shape the future with confidence."

| Flagship Series | Cadence | Description |
|---|---|---|

| EY Megatrends | Long-horizon | Structural trends reshaping business strategy. |

| Private Equity Pulse | Quarterly | PE dealmaking, fundraising, and value creation. |

## Notable History

- **Project Everest** — the proposed split of EY's audit and consulting businesses into two distinct organizations — was publicly announced in 2022 and **cancelled in April 2023**. EY continues to operate as an integrated multi-line professional services organization.
